Six basic human needs


Tony here will help people understand their 6 basic human needs. He emphasizes this in relationship issues and has this at the center of a lot of his interventions.
According to this human needs psychology, the 6 needs are:

  1. Certainty - consistency, stability, knowing what comes next
  2. Variety - excitement, differences, unknown risk
  3. Significance - having a purpose, sense of importance, uniqueness
  4. Love and Connection - feeling of closeness, communication, intimacy
  5. Growth - learning, development, progression in life
  6. Contribution - giving to others, serving higher purpose, caring for others
