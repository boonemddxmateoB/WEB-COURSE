Week 1

This is the weekly training schedule to build a base level of strength. The idea is to start out light weight, and add 5 lbs. to each exercize every time you do them. If you can't do the newest weight, then you can just complete what you can.

Week 2

The idea is to build every time as much as you can without getting injury. This allows your body to build supportive muscle to complete the exercize. Alternating the routines consistently, keeping track of where you are at, and being diligent about doing the routines will allow your body to grow.
